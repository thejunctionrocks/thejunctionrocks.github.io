bio.

We are THE JUNCTION, from Padova, Italy.
Marco Simioni (guitar, vocals), Francesco Reffo (drums, vocals) and Alessandro Maroso (bass, backing vocals).
We play indie rock with garage punk attitude and noise pop taste.
